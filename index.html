Xiang Xu (徐翔)

I am an assistant professor in Shandong University of Finance and Economics. I received my Ph.D. degree in the Department of Software Engineering at Shandong University of China in 2021, working under the supervision of Prof. Chenglei Yang, co-supervised by Prof. Lu Wang, Prof. Philipp Slusallek. My research interests include high-performance rendering, photorealistic rendering, and a little bit machine learning.

xuxiang@sdufe.edu.cn
